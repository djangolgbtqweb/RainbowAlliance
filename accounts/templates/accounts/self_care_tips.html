Self-Care Tips

Explore self-care strategies for mental and emotional well-being.

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation daily.
  • Engage in physical activities like yoga or walking.
  • Stay connected with supportive friends and family.
  • Prioritize rest and maintain a consistent sleep schedule.
  • Engage in hobbies that bring joy and relaxation.
